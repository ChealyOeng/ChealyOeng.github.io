Triumph

Triumph (Screen-print & Paint on Fabric) Conquering hardships is the journey of growth. This piece was created during the time when I was about to leave high school, home, and ’expand my wings’ to explore the next chapter in life.

Made with a combination of screen-printing and painting on fabric in large scale bold colour, with the intention of depicting a grand look.

Kelly Fan 2022 - 2024
